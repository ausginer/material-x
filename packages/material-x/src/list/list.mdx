## Anatomy

- `mx-list`: container for list items.
- `mx-list-item`: static content item.
- `mx-list-button-item`: native button-backed action item.
- `mx-list-link-item`: native anchor-backed navigation item.
- `start` and `end` slots: leading and trailing affordances.
- `supporting-text`, `overline`, and `trailing-supporting-text` slots:
  secondary text regions.

## Material 3 reference and deltas

- Material 3 overview:
  [https://m3.material.io/components/lists/overview](https://m3.material.io/components/lists/overview)
- Deltas:
  - One-line is the default; denser rows are inferred from slotted overline and
    supporting text.
  - Reveal, reorder, expandable, segmented-list behavior, and dividers are not
    included in this first implementation.
